Drawing Program

Draw a Shape!

This program was created on Snap. You can plot as many points as you want and it will connect the points to draw a shape! You can also draw an "A" by clicking the green flag. See what awesome shapes you can come up with!

Scratch Launcher Game

Play This Game

This is a game I made on Scratch! You have to aviod the fortune cookies and reach the finishline, but each time you reach it another fortune cookie is added! Catch the taco wizard for lots of extra points! This game can be played by two people. Challenge a friend to see who can get the most points!

Draw Some Fun!

Try it out!

In this program you can draw custom shapes, polygons, and you can free draw with a pen tool! Learn what different polygons look like and use your creativity to draw your own shapes! You can change background colors and pen colors too! You can also fill a polygon, custom shape, or free drawn shape with color.
